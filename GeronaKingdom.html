Gerona Kingdom Chapters

Gerona Kingdom is a country with massive territory that spans across a large portion of the earth and in which that many cultures formed, people living their lives and strange mysteries in the shadows for the citizens to discover and solve!

Many characters inhabit the wonderous and magical land of Gerona, each with their own story to tell. Each book is it's stand-alone story so you can pick a character that appeals to you the most and jump in there. If you would like to see the characters of Gerona, click here!
